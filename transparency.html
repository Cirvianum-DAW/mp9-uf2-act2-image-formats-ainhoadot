Question 1: What is the most optimal format for this type of image and why?

Answer: For this type of image, the PNG(32-bit) format would be the most optimal, as it provides a good ratio between quality, file size and does not lose transparency.

Question 2: What happens when we save it in JPEG format?

Answer: When saved in JPEG format, the image undergoes lossy compression, which leads to a decrease in quality, especially in detailed parts.

Question 3: If our website has a white background, which format would be the most optimal and why?

Answer: If our website has a white background, the PNG format (32-bit) would be the most optimal, as it offers good image quality without lossy compression, and the white background can be displayed clearly.
